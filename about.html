Our Journey

From One Rickshaw
to a Trusted Name

It all started in 2024 in Uluberia — with a single e-rickshaw and a simple belief: that clean, affordable electric vehicles could change lives. What began as a small family venture has steadily grown into a dealership that communities trust.


Today, we proudly serve customers across Uluberia, Moubesia, Dhulasimla, and 58 Gate, with over 150 happy customers who rely on our vehicles every day. Our commitment goes beyond the sale — with genuine spare parts, honest advice, and dependable after-sales service, we make sure your EV keeps running as it should. Every family we serve feels like our own.

Top 5 Benefits of Switching to an
E-Rickshaw in West Bengal

West Bengal is one of India's fastest growing states for electric vehicles. Rising petrol prices and worsening air pollution are pushing thousands of drivers to make the switch. At Rit Motor, we've helped hundreds of customers transform their livelihoods — and the numbers tell a powerful story.

1
💰 Save Money on Fuel — Every Single Day
₹8,000–₹10,000 saved per month

A typical petrol auto rickshaw costs ₹300–400 per day just in fuel. An e-rickshaw? Just ₹20–30 to fully charge overnight. That's a saving of over ₹8,000 every month — or nearly ₹1 lakh per year.

Over 3 years, the fuel savings alone more than pay for the entire vehicle. Most of our customers break even within 12–18 months of purchasing an e-rickshaw from Rit Motor.

2
🌿 Zero Pollution — A Cleaner West Bengal
Zero direct tailpipe emissions

E-rickshaws produce zero direct emissions. No exhaust fumes, no carbon monoxide, no particulate matter. For drivers spending 8–10 hours on the road daily, this is a massive health benefit too.

Many cities across West Bengal — including parts of Kolkata and Howrah — are restricting petrol vehicles in certain zones. E-rickshaws are always permitted, giving you access to more routes and more earnings.

3
🔧 Very Low Maintenance Costs
70% lower maintenance vs petrol

Petrol autos have engines, gear systems, carburettors, spark plugs, and oil filters — all of which need regular servicing. E-rickshaws have a simple electric motor with very few moving parts.

  • No engine oil changes
  • No spark plugs or fuel filters
  • No clutch or gearbox repairs
  • Brake pads last much longer due to regenerative braking

At Rit Motor, we stock genuine spare parts for all e-rickshaw models we sell. Our service team in Uluberia is always available for any maintenance support.

4
🏛️ Government Support & Subsidies
FAME II + State scheme benefits

The Indian government's FAME II scheme provides direct subsidies on electric vehicles, including e-rickshaws. West Bengal also has its own state-level EV promotion scheme.

Benefits available include:

  • Upfront purchase subsidy reducing vehicle cost
  • Reduced road tax for EV owners
  • Priority permit allocation in many districts
  • Easier bank loan approval for EV purchases

Our team at Rit Motor will guide you through every available scheme — ensuring you get maximum benefit at the time of purchase.

5
📈 Higher Daily Net Earnings
₹500–800 more profit per month

Because your running costs are dramatically lower, your net profit per trip goes up — even if you charge the same fare as petrol autos. Our customers consistently report earning ₹500–800 more per month after switching.

With the money saved on fuel and maintenance, many drivers are able to repay their vehicle loan faster, upgrade their home, or invest in a second e-rickshaw for their family.

The Verdict

Switching to an e-rickshaw is one of the best financial decisions a driver in West Bengal can make today. Lower running costs, zero pollution, government support, and higher profits — the case is clear. The question is not whether to switch, but when.

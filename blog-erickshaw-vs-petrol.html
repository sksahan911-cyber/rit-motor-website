E-Rickshaw vs Petrol Auto: Which is Better for
West Bengal Roads in 2024?

This is the most common question we hear at Expreso — "Should I buy an e-rickshaw or stick with a petrol auto?" We're going to give you a completely honest, numbers-based comparison so you can make the right decision for your livelihood.

Quick Comparison Table

Category 🛺 E-Rickshaw 🚗 Petrol Auto Winner
Daily Running Cost ₹20–30 (charging) ₹350–450 (petrol) ✅ E-Rickshaw
Purchase Price ₹1.5L–2L ₹2.5L–3.5L ✅ E-Rickshaw
Monthly Maintenance Very Low (₹500–1,000) High (₹2,000–4,000) ✅ E-Rickshaw
Govt. Support Heavy subsidies (FAME II) No support ✅ E-Rickshaw
WB Road Performance Smooth, quiet, city-perfect Noisy, high maintenance ✅ E-Rickshaw
Future-Proof Always permitted Being banned in zones ✅ E-Rickshaw
💰 Round 1: Daily Running Cost
✅ E-Rickshaw Wins
E-Rickshaw
₹20–30 per day to charge overnight
Petrol Auto
₹350–450 per day on petrol

The difference is staggering — e-rickshaw drivers save over ₹8,000–₹10,000 every month on fuel alone. Over a year, that's nearly ₹1 lakh straight into your pocket.

🏷️ Round 2: Purchase Price
✅ E-Rickshaw Wins
E-Rickshaw
₹1.5 lakh – ₹2 lakh (with govt subsidy, even less)
Petrol Auto
₹2.5 lakh – ₹3.5 lakh (no subsidy)

E-rickshaws are significantly cheaper to purchase — and with FAME II and West Bengal state subsidies, the effective cost drops even further. At Expreso, we help you access every available scheme.

🔧 Round 3: Maintenance
✅ E-Rickshaw Wins
E-Rickshaw
Simple motor, very few parts. Monthly cost ₹500–1,000
Petrol Auto
Engine, gear, carburetor, spark plugs. ₹2,000–4,000/month

Electric motors have fewer than 20 moving parts. Petrol engines have hundreds. Less to go wrong means less money spent on repairs. Genuine e-rickshaw spare parts are always available at our Expreso store.

🏛️ Round 4: Government Support
✅ E-Rickshaw Wins
E-Rickshaw
FAME II subsidy + WB state scheme + lower road tax + priority permits
Petrol Auto
No government support. Higher taxes.

The Indian government is actively incentivising EV adoption. Petrol vehicles receive no support and face increasing restrictions. The policy direction is very clear.

🛣️ Round 5: West Bengal Road Performance
✅ E-Rickshaw Wins
E-Rickshaw
Smooth, quiet, instant torque — perfect for stop-go city traffic
Petrol Auto
Loud, hot, struggles in slow traffic. Stalls in heavy congestion.

West Bengal's roads — especially in Howrah, Uluberia, and Kolkata — involve constant stop-start driving. E-rickshaws excel in exactly this environment. No stalling, no overheating, no gear changes.

🔮 Round 6: Future-Proof
✅ E-Rickshaw Wins
E-Rickshaw
Always permitted. Expanding routes. Government backed.
Petrol Auto
Being banned in city zones. Future uncertain.

Petrol autos are being restricted in more and more areas across West Bengal and India. An e-rickshaw gives you access to every route — now and in the future — without the risk of permit cancellation or zone bans.

The Verdict

The numbers speak for themselves. On every metric that matters — running costs, purchase price, maintenance, government support, road performance, and future viability — the e-rickshaw wins. For West Bengal drivers in 2024, the e-rickshaw is not just the smarter choice, it's the only choice that makes financial sense.
